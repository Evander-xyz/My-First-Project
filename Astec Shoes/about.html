Our Story


Aztec is one of the most-trusted athletic footwear and apparel retail chains in the United States. What began as a small, family business has now grown into an extremely-popular chain of stores and an online retail site


Aztec Shoes was built on a strong belief that amazing buyer service, above all else, is the most-important reason why any business flourishes.Astec prides itself on offering its customers the Ultimate Experience when it comes to their shoe and apparel shopping. Our locations, staff, and products are all the very best imaginable.Aztec carries only elite shoe and apparel brands. Our chic and loyal clientele reflect that commitment to retail excellence.


The Foundation


The Yamez clan founded Aztec in 1999. A single brick and mortar location in San Jose, CA started it all. The Aztec brand was built on character, commuitment, and ingenuity. Presently,Aztec retail locations are located throughout the Southwest, and we are constantly expanding. That amazing growth from a single, humble location to over 160 stores was accomplished by dedication, hard work, and a commitment to providing a warm, family-type atmosphere for our customers.


Our Values


From the very start, Astec separated itself from the competition by making sales secondary to the customer. SP puts the consumer on a pedestal because they have been so instrumental in making us the huge success we are today. We would not be in our current position without the strength of our supporter's loyalty.

To ensure that every one of our Aztec loyalists has a rewarding experience every time they step foot into one of our locations, we have hired employees who understand and uphold what Aztec was built upon. Our faithful and hard-working staff is an extension of the Astec culture.

Aztec realizes that awesome customer service alone cannot make a business prosperous. We have also dedicated ourselves to carrying only the premium and in-demand footwear and apparel. We have an eye for the hottest styles and trends because we have an interest in the merchandise we sell.


Our Culture



The yamez family chose athletic footwear and apparel as their profession because they knew that market and had a passion for it. The Aztec customers are chic and fanatical about their shoes and apparel. We make sure to supply them with the most stylish brand names available.
